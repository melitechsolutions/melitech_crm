# Melitech CRM - Quick Start Guide

**Get up and running in 15 minutes!**

---

## 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Log In (1 minute)

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to your Melitech CRM URL
3. Click **"Sign in to Continue"**
4. Enter your email and password
5. Complete any security verification if prompted

### Step 2: Update Your Profile (2 minutes)

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right corner)
2. Select **"Profile Settings"**
3. Add your:
   - Full Name
   - Department
   - Job Title
   - Phone Number
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Step 3: Explore the Dashboard (2 minutes)

1. You're now on your **Dashboard**
2. Notice the main sections:
   - **Sales Overview** (top-left)
   - **Financial Summary** (top-right)
   - **Project Status** (middle)
   - **Recent Activity** (bottom)
3. Click on any section to drill down into details

## Your First Tasks

### Task 1: Add Your First Client

**Time: 3 minutes**

1. Click **Clients** in the left menu
2. Click **"Add New Client"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Client Name**: "Acme Corporation"
   - **Email**: "contact@acme.com"
   - **Phone**: "555-123-4567"
   - **Industry**: Select from dropdown
4. Click **"Save Client"**

✅ **Done!** You've added your first client.

### Task 2: Create Your First Invoice

**Time: 5 minutes**

1. Click **Invoices** in the left menu
2. Click **"Create New Invoice"**
3. Select **Client**: "Acme Corporation" (the one you just added)
4. Fill in:
   - **Invoice Date**: Today's date
   - **Due Date**: 30 days from today
5. Click **"Add Line Item"**
6. Enter:
   - **Description**: "Consulting Services"
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Unit Price**: 1000
7. Click **"Save Invoice"**

✅ **Done!** You've created your first invoice.

### Task 3: Send the Invoice to Your Client

**Time: 2 minutes**

1. Find the invoice you just created
2. Click **"Send Invoice"**
3. Verify the email address is correct
4. Click **"Send"**

✅ **Done!** Your client will receive the invoice.

## Key Features You Need to Know

### 1. Search Everything

The search bar at the top lets you find anything:
- Type a client name to find all their records
- Type an invoice number to find a specific invoice
- Type an employee name to find their profile

### 2. Filter and Sort

Most lists have filter options:
- Click **"Filters"** to narrow down results
- Click column headers to sort by that column
- Use **"Saved Filters"** for filters you use often

### 3. Export Data

Need data in Excel? Most lists have an **"Export"** button:
- Click **"Export"**
- Choose format (Excel or CSV)
- Open the file in Excel or Google Sheets

### 4. Notifications

Stay informed with notifications:
- Click the **bell icon** (top-right) to see alerts
- Go to **Settings** → **Notifications** to configure what you receive

### 5. Mobile Access

Use your phone or tablet:
- Open any web browser
- Navigate to your CRM URL
- The interface automatically adjusts for mobile

## Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Track a Sale from Start to Finish

**Step 1**: Create an **Opportunity**
- Go to **Opportunities** → **"Add Opportunity"**
- Enter the potential deal value and client

**Step 2**: Move through the pipeline
- As the deal progresses, drag it through stages
- Update the probability as you get closer to closing

**Step 3**: Convert to an Invoice
- When you win the deal, create an **Invoice**
- Reference the opportunity in the invoice description

**Step 4**: Track the Payment
- When the client pays, record it in **Payments**
- The system automatically marks the invoice as paid

### Workflow 2: Manage a Project

**Step 1**: Create a **Project**
- Go to **Projects** → **"Create Project"**
- Set the start date, end date, and budget

**Step 2**: Add Tasks
- Click **"Add Task"** for each project task
- Assign tasks to team members
- Set due dates and priorities

**Step 3**: Track Progress
- Team members update task status as they work
- View the **Progress Bar** to see overall completion
- Check the **Timeline** to see if you're on schedule

**Step 4**: Complete the Project
- Mark all tasks as complete
- Archive the project when finished

### Workflow 3: Process Payroll

**Step 1**: Go to **Payroll** → **"Create Payroll"**

**Step 2**: Select the pay period (monthly, bi-weekly, etc.)

**Step 3**: Review employee salaries and make adjustments

**Step 4**: Click **"Calculate Payroll"**

**Step 5**: Review the summary and click **"Process Payroll"**

**Step 6**: Generate payslips for employees

## Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

| Problem | Quick Fix |
|---|---|
| **Page won't load** | Refresh (F5) or clear browser cache |
| **Can't find a record** | Use search bar or check filters |
| **Save button doesn't work** | Check that all required fields (*) are filled |
| **Slow performance** | Close other browser tabs or restart browser |
| **Permission denied** | Contact your administrator for access |
