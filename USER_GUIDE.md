## Getting Started

Welcome to Melitech Solutions CRM! This comprehensive guide will help you understand and effectively use all features of the system. Whether you're a business owner, manager, or team member, this guide provides step-by-step instructions for every task.

### What is Melitech CRM?

Melitech CRM is a complete business management solution designed to help companies streamline operations across sales, finance, human resources, and project management. The system centralizes all your business data in one secure location, making it easy for your team to collaborate and make informed decisions.

### Key Benefits

The Melitech CRM provides several important advantages for your business:

**Centralized Data Management**: All customer information, financial records, employee data, and project details are stored in one secure system, eliminating the need for multiple spreadsheets and databases.

**Real-Time Collaboration**: Your team can work together seamlessly with instant updates and notifications, ensuring everyone has access to the latest information.

**Comprehensive Reporting**: Generate detailed reports and analytics to understand your business performance and make data-driven decisions.

**Automated Workflows**: Reduce manual work by automating routine tasks like invoice generation, payment reminders, and leave approvals.

**Mobile-Friendly Interface**: Access your CRM from any device with a web browser, whether you're in the office or on the go.

## Initial Setup

### Step 1: Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser and navigate to your Melitech CRM URL (provided by your administrator)
2. You should see the login page with the Melitech Solutions CRM logo
3. If you don't have login credentials, contact your system administrator

### Step 2: First Login

Follow these steps to log in to your account:

1. Click the **"Sign in to Continue"** button on the login page
2. You will be redirected to the Manus authentication portal
3. Enter your email address and password
4. If this is your first time, you may need to set up two-factor authentication (2FA) for security
5. After successful authentication, you will be redirected back to the CRM dashboard

### Step 3: Updating Your Profile

After your first login, it's important to complete your user profile:

1. Click on your **profile icon** in the top-right corner of the screen
2. Select **"Profile Settings"** from the dropdown menu
3. Update the following information:
   - Full Name
   - Department
   - Job Title
   - Phone Number
   - Profile Picture (optional)
4. Click **"Save Changes"** to update your profile

### Step 4: Setting User Preferences

Customize your CRM experience by adjusting your preferences:

1. Go to **Settings** → **User Preferences**
2. Configure the following options:
   - **Theme**: Choose between Light and Dark mode
   - **Date Format**: Select your preferred date display format
   - **Time Zone**: Set your local time zone for accurate timestamps
   - **Language**: Choose your preferred language (if available)
   - **Email Notifications**: Configure which notifications you want to receive
3. Click **"Save Preferences"** to apply your changes

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your home page in the Melitech CRM. It provides a quick overview of important information and quick access to key features.

The dashboard is organized into several sections:

## Core Modules

Melitech CRM includes 21 comprehensive modules covering all aspects of business management. Here's an overview of each module:

### 1. Clients Module

The Clients module helps you manage all customer relationships and information.

**Adding a New Client**

1. Navigate to **Clients** from the main menu
2. Click the **"Add New Client"** button
3. Fill in the following information:
   - **Client Name**: The company or individual name
   - **Email Address**: Primary contact email
   - **Phone Number**: Contact phone number
   - **Address**: Full business address
   - **Industry**: Select the industry type
   - **Website**: Company website (optional)
   - **Contact Person**: Name of primary contact
4. Click **"Save Client"** to create the record

**Viewing Client Details**

1. Go to **Clients** and find the client you want to view
2. Click on the client name to open their profile
3. You can see:
   - Complete contact information
   - All associated invoices and payments
   - Active projects and opportunities
   - Communication history
   - Custom notes and attachments

**Updating Client Information**

1. Open the client profile
2. Click the **"Edit"** button
3. Modify the information as needed
4. Click **"Save Changes"** to update the record

### 2. Invoices Module

The Invoices module allows you to create, send, and track customer invoices.

**Creating an Invoice**

1. Navigate to **Invoices** from the main menu
2. Click **"Create New Invoice"**
3. Select the **Client** from the dropdown list
4. Enter the following information:
   - **Invoice Number**: Auto-generated or custom
   - **Invoice Date**: Date the invoice is issued
   - **Due Date**: When payment is due
   - **Description**: Brief description of services/products
5. Add line items:
   - Click **"Add Line Item"**
   - Enter **Description**, **Quantity**, and **Unit Price**
   - The system automatically calculates the total
6. Review the invoice and click **"Save Invoice"**

**Sending an Invoice**

1. Open the invoice you want to send
2. Click the **"Send Invoice"** button
3. Verify the client's email address
4. Add a personal message (optional)
5. Click **"Send"** to email the invoice to the client

**Tracking Invoice Status**

1. Go to **Invoices** and view your invoice list
2. Each invoice shows its status:
   - **Draft**: Not yet sent
   - **Sent**: Sent to client but not paid
   - **Partially Paid**: Some payment received
   - **Paid**: Fully paid
   - **Overdue**: Payment past due date
3. Click on an invoice to view payment history and details

## Best Practices

Following these best practices will help you get the most out of your Melitech CRM:

### Data Entry Best Practices

**Be Consistent with Data Entry**: Use consistent formatting for names, addresses, and other information. For example, always use "New York, NY" instead of sometimes using "New York" and sometimes "NY".

**Complete All Required Fields**: Fill in all mandatory fields when creating records. This ensures you have complete information for reporting and analysis.

**Use Clear Descriptions**: Write clear, descriptive notes and comments. Future users (including yourself) will appreciate detailed information about why decisions were made.

**Regular Data Cleanup**: Periodically review your data for duplicates, outdated information, and errors. Clean data leads to accurate reports and better decision-making.

### Financial Management Best Practices

**Invoice Promptly**: Send invoices immediately after completing work or delivering products. Prompt invoicing improves cash flow.

**Follow Up on Overdue Payments**: Use the system's reporting features to identify overdue invoices and follow up with clients promptly.

**Record All Expenses**: Maintain accurate expense records for tax purposes and financial analysis. Every expense should be recorded in the system.

**Reconcile Regularly**: Compare your system records with bank statements monthly to ensure accuracy.

**Monitor Cash Flow**: Review your financial dashboard regularly to understand your cash position and plan accordingly.

### Project Management Best Practices

**Set Clear Milestones**: Break projects into clear milestones with specific dates. This helps track progress and identify delays early.

**Assign Clear Responsibilities**: Ensure each task is assigned to a specific person. Unclear ownership leads to tasks being overlooked.

**Update Status Regularly**: Keep task statuses current. This helps the team understand what's happening and identify bottlenecks.

**Communicate Changes**: When project scope, timeline, or budget changes, update the system immediately and notify the team.

**Review Progress Weekly**: Hold regular project reviews to discuss progress, identify issues, and adjust plans as needed.

### Customer Relationship Best Practices

**Maintain Accurate Client Information**: Keep client contact information current. Outdated information leads to communication failures.

**Track All Interactions**: Record all communications with clients in the system. This creates a complete history and helps other team members understand the relationship.

**Follow Up Promptly**: Respond to client inquiries and requests quickly. Good follow-up builds trust and improves customer satisfaction.

**Personalize Communications**: Use client information to personalize communications. Clients appreciate being treated as individuals, not just account numbers.

**Regular Check-ins**: Periodically reach out to clients to ensure they're satisfied and to identify new opportunities.

### Team Collaboration Best Practices

**Use Comments and Notes**: Use the system's commenting features to communicate about specific records. This keeps all relevant information in one place.

**Share Relevant Information**: Make sure team members have access to information they need to do their jobs. Proper access control is important for security.

**Provide Training**: Ensure all team members understand how to use the system effectively. Well-trained users provide better data quality.

**Document Processes**: Create written procedures for common tasks. This helps new team members learn quickly and ensures consistency.

**Regular Feedback**: Gather feedback from users about what's working well and what could be improved.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

**Issue: I can't log in to the system**

**Solution**: 
1. Verify you're using the correct email address
2. Check that Caps Lock is not on
3. If you forgot your password, click **"Forgot Password"** on the login page
4. Check your email for the password reset link
5. If you still can't log in, contact your system administrator

**Issue: The page is loading very slowly**

**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection speed
2. Try refreshing the page (press F5)
3. Clear your browser cache:
   - Chrome: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete, select "All time", click "Clear data"
   - Firefox: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete, select "Everything", click "Clear Now"
4. Try using a different browser
5. Contact your administrator if the problem persists

**Issue: I can't find a record I created**

**Solution**:
1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
2. Try using different search terms
3. Check if you're looking in the correct module
4. Verify the record wasn't deleted
5. Try clearing filters that might be hiding the record

**Issue: An error message appears when I try to save**

**Solution**:
1. Check that all required fields are filled in (marked with *)
2. Verify that dates are in the correct format
3. Check that numbers don't contain letters or special characters
4. Try refreshing the page and entering the data again
5. Contact your administrator if the error persists

**Issue: I don't have permission to access a module**

**Solution**:
1. Contact your system administrator to request access
2. Verify you have the correct role assigned to your account
3. Check if the module is enabled in your system configuration

**Issue: My changes aren't being saved**

**Solution**:
1. Make sure you click the **"Save"** button after making changes
2. Check for error messages that might explain why the save failed
3. Verify you have permission to edit the record
4. Try refreshing the page and making the changes again
5. Contact your administrator if the problem continues

## FAQ

**Q: How do I change my password?**

A: Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner, select "Profile Settings", then click "Change Password". Enter your current password and your new password twice, then click "Save".

**Q: Can I access the CRM from my mobile phone?**

A: Yes, the Melitech CRM is designed to work on mobile devices. Simply open your web browser and navigate to your CRM URL. The interface will automatically adjust for your phone screen.

**Q: How often should I back up my data?**

A: The system automatically backs up your data daily. However, you can also create manual backups from Settings → Backup & Security. We recommend keeping at least one backup copy stored outside your computer.

**Q: What should I do if I accidentally delete a record?**

A: Contact your system administrator immediately. Depending on your backup settings, they may be able to restore the deleted record. This is why regular backups are important.

**Q: How do I export data from the CRM?**

A: Most list views have an "Export" button. Click it to download data as Excel or CSV format. You can then open the file in Excel or other spreadsheet applications.

**Q: Can multiple people work on the same project at the same time?**

A: Yes, multiple team members can work on the same project simultaneously. The system updates in real-time, so everyone sees the latest information.

**Q: How do I set up automatic invoice reminders?**

A: Go to Settings → Notifications and enable "Invoice Reminders". You can configure how many days before the due date you want to be reminded.

**Q: What's the difference between an Estimate and an Invoice?**

A: An Estimate is a quote you send to a potential customer. An Invoice is a bill you send after work is completed or products are delivered. You can convert an accepted Estimate into an Invoice.

**Q: How do I create a report?**

A: Most modules have a "Reports" section. Select the report type you want, choose your date range and filters, then click "Generate Report". You can view the report on screen or export it as PDF or Excel.

**Q: Who can approve leave requests?**

A: Typically, an employee's direct manager approves their leave requests. Administrators can also approve leave requests. Contact your manager or administrator to confirm who has approval authority.

**Q: How do I add a new user to the system?**

A: Go to Settings → User Management, click "Add User", enter their email address, select their role, and click "Send Invitation". They will receive an email with instructions to set up their account.

**Q: What happens if I don't update my profile?**

A: Your profile is used for system communications and reporting. While the system will still work, it's best to keep your profile current so others can contact you and your information is accurate in reports.

**Q: Can I customize the dashboard to show different information?**

A: Yes, click "Customize Dashboard" to select which widgets you want to display and rearrange them as needed.

**Q: How do I know if my internet connection is fast enough?**

A: The CRM requires a minimum of 2 Mbps download speed. You can test your connection at speedtest.net. If your connection is slower, contact your internet service provider.
